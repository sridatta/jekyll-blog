In my previous post, I use masculine pronouns to describe those in the tech industry. This is because "she/he" is an unwieldy construct and the singular "they" is grammatically questionable. I could make a concession and refer to them solely as "she" but tokenism can be easily recognized as such and distracts from the writing.

As long as masculine pronouns describe an alarming majority of my peers, I will use them. But I do hope that one day the word choice will not be as clear-cut.
